About Me

Hi! I’m Jonathan Gunasingham, an 8th-year high school chemistry teacher in St. Louis County. I have a Bachelor of Applied Science and a Bachelor of Education, both from the University of Toronto. I am currently pursuing a Master’s of Science in Learning Technologies and Design at the University of Missouri-Columbia. After school, I coach a high school robotics team and ultimate frisbee team.
I am passionate about creating relevant learning experiences and community for students. I attempt this with the same attitude of Toby Ziegler from The West Wing:

“We’re a group. We’re a team...We win together, we lose together. We celebrate and we mourn together. And defeats are softened and victories sweeter because we did them together… You’re my guys and I’m yours, and there’s nothing I wouldn’t do for you”.
I am excited to see how my graduate classes will enhance my skills in these two areas.
